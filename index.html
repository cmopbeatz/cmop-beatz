cmopbeatz.com

Welcome to cmopbeatz.com!

Thank you for taking the time to stop in. We hope you enjoy our catalogue and above all else--build your next hit with us! Whether you're looking for good filler music or looking to finish your album, we've got just about anything you need! Of course, if you disagree, please contact us and we'll MAKE whatever you need!

We're striving to contribute to the music community and education in a positive way. cmopbeatz.com will be donating a portion of our proceeds each quarter to vh1's Save The Music Foundation. For more info, click "About cmopbeatz.com," located at the bottom of the page.

We are also, constantly on the lookout for new talent/music to add to our catalogue. If you like our sound, and are interested in collaborating, or simply looking for production/exclusive original material, you may contact us directly (a short email directory is located at the bottom of the page).

Once again, welcome and enjoy!

save the music

cmopbeatz.com proudly supports the arts and their vital role in education. That's why every quarter, we will donate 15% of our sales revenue toward preserving music's place in education.

vh1's "Save The Music Foundation" is a 501c3 nonprofit organization dedicated to restoring music programs in America’s public schools, and raising awareness about the importance of music as part of each child’s complete education. For information on how to get involved, or to donate directly, please visit:
